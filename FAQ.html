FAQ


General


What is Dominionathon?

Dominionathon is a Dominion tournament being held this summer in Philadelphia, PA.
It will be a full day tournament with prizes, trophies, and fun to be had.


When is it?

Saturday, July 25 2015. Doors open at 12:00 PM.


Where is it?

The venue is the Maas Building in Northern Liberties, easily accessible via SEPTA (Market Frankford Line).
There is also plenty of street parking if you plan to drive.

The Maas Building
1325 N Randolph St
Philadelphia, PA 19122



Who should attend?

Do you like Dominion? Then you should come. Do you like winning trohies? Then you should come. Do you like testing your skills against
local people who share your interests? Then you should come. Do you want to sharpen your skills and learn some new tricks? Then you
should come. Do you just want to play a lot of Dominion with local people who share the same interests as you? Then you should come.

Please not that since Dominionathon is a BYOB event, you must be at least 21 years old to attend.


Registration


How do I register?

You can register here on our website, or at the door at the event.

Early registration is highly recommended as space is limited to the first 100 registrants. (Note: RSVP-ing on our Facebook page -- although
highly encouraged -- is not the same thing as registering, which can only be done via this link or at the door, if space is still available.) If you
would like to register but are unable to use Paypal, please contact us at dominionathon@gmail.com to make alternative arrangements.


How much does it cost?

Registration costs $15 until July 5th. After that and at the door, registration will cost $20.


What am I paying for?

Great Question! Your registration fee grants you all-day access to the event/facility and an opportunity to hoist your very own Dominionathon
trophy! There will be ample opportunity to compete in many rounds of Dominion before we start culling the herd. And even if you eventually do get
eliminated from competition, our 100+ game open-gaming library will ensure that the fun doesn't stop till midnight!
